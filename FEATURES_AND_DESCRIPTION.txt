
                    LEGALEASE - LEGAL DOCUMENT & COMPLIANCE ADVISOR
                            COMPREHENSIVE FEATURE LIST

WHAT THIS WEBSITE DOES:

LegalEase is an AI-powered legal assistance web application designed to help 
users understand legal documents, compliance requirements, and legal concepts 
in simple, layman-friendly language. It uses Google's Gemini AI to provide 
structured analysis, risk assessments, and actionable recommendations for 
various legal matters.

The platform serves as an educational and advisory tool (NOT a substitute for 
professional legal counsel) that helps users:
- Analyze legal documents and contracts
- Understand legal terminology
- Get answers to legal questions
- Access legal document templates
- Format legal citations
- Categorize legal matters by area of law
- Assess risks and receive recommendations
